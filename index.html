To my lovely wife to be

Open Me

Bangaram ninnu entha la miss avutunano kuda cheppaleka potunna nenu – I just miss you very badly and you can’t even imagine how much.

Ninnu eppudaite preminchadam start chesano, appati nunchi na life entha happy ga better ga undo telusa. Nuvu navvite naku chala baaga anipistundi; nuvu badhapadite, asal chudalenu nenu; nuvu ediste, asal tattukolenu nenu.

Naku konni sarlu feelings chupinchadam raadu, Nishu – I can’t express some feelings, but I feel everything that’s happening to you whether it’s happy or sad. You have been with me from the beginning till now and will be in the future. I can’t imagine a better future without you in my life. I want you to be my life.

Na heart ni kosame, na prema ni kosame, prathi okka alochana anni ni kosame, Nishu. Nuvu jagrathaga unte naku inka em avasaram ledu, adi chalu.

Nuvu lekunda picchi odini avtunna, telusa nenu – mental ekkestundi ee madhya. Every moment, nuvve gurtostunnav (I keep remembering you every moment).

Nenu ninnu chupimani adugutanu kada (you know what I mean) – chudali anipisthundi nenu, like I want to see you completely. I know nenu ni deggara unte ade possible avtadi ani, and online lo privacy sarigga undadu and aa intimacy undadu. Kani emo adagalanipinchindi naku, so nenu ekuva sarlu adigi ninnu bother ches tunna anipinchindi.

I’ll be more myself from now on, but I can’t keep my mind off things sometimes. Kani okati matram gurtupettuko Nishu – I’ll always be there for you, whether in happiness or sadness, sickness or health. I’ll always, always be there for you, bangaram.

I miss you so much. I love you, my queen. ❤️
